Where we Started...

Just to give a bit a insight as to how this website came about, I've been a long time fan of sports, sepcifically american football named the NFL. My favorite NFL team is the Green Bay Packers and i've been a fan since about 2012! Football has always been a passion of mine but up until about 2019 I started to get more into basketball. Friends of mine have been lifetime basketball fans but it never really intrigued me the same way it did football. A big contributing factor in me wanting to know more about basketball is the video game franchise NBA2k. I'm a huge gamer and playing the NBA2k games over the past handuful of years really put into prespective how much really went into basketball! My goal for this website is to inform new basketball fans, people intrested in basketball, and even maybe giving experienced fans information about the sport they might have not known previously.

I hope that everyone that visits learns at LEAST one thing from here. WELCOME TO HOOPS FOR DUMMIES!!!!!!
